About Us

Who are we?

Nightline Travel is a cutting-edge airline that specializes in overnight flights, providing passengers with a unique travel experience that maximizes convenience and efficiency. The airline has strategically designed its services to cater to night owls and those seeking to make the most of their time.

What we do?

To enhance the travel experience, Nightline Travel features state-of-the-art sleeper cabins. Passengers can enjoy the comfort of lie-flat beds, soft ambient lighting, and noise-cancelling headphones to create a serene environment conducive to restful sleep. Each cabin is equipped with personal entertainment screens, providing a selection of movies, TV shows, and music to make the journey enjoyable.

For you

For passengers with layovers, Nightline Travel provides an exclusive nighttime lounge at select airports. This lounge offers a tranquil atmosphere, comfortable seating, and refreshments, allowing travelers to relax and recharge during their connection. Nightline Travel revolutionizes air travel by capitalizing on the benefits of overnight flights, making it the go-to choice for those who value time, comfort, and a unique travel experience.
